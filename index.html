Intro

This Website is a virtual photo album containing the many screenshots i have taken while exploring the vast world of Red Dead Redemption 2.

The world is huge, and filled with incredible details large and small. The world is rich and diverserse with animals, and characters, many of whom you can interact with as you explore mountains to the north, all the way down to the deserts in the south. the crocodile filled swamps to the west as well as the dense game rich forrests to the east. there is many oppertunities for beautiful and interesting photograpphs while exploring the world, and this is a collection of the photos i took during my playhrough.

About

I am currently playing Red Read Redemption 2, and because it's so incredibly beautiful, i find myself taking a lot of screenshots. Over 100 now in total. I am also currently studying Web Design in school, so this website is a personal project to practice my web design skills, I worked with a template from html5up.net, and then modified it to meet my design requirements.

As a novice web designer, i find i learn a lot from studying and modifying pre made templates. Going forward i aim to learn the required skills to write these websites myself, and hopefully once i am finished my course i will be well on the way to achieving that goal. I will also be studying Java Script as part of my course, which i am looking forward to, as i currently don't understand that aspect of these html5up.net templates, however as my course continues, i am sure a lot of these features will become clear to me. Anyway, i hope you enjoy my site, and the photographs contained within.
